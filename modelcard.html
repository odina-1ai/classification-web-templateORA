Flower Classification

Purpose

Meant to be used as an educational tool for users to determine whether flowers are posionous or not. Current limitations:
Flowers can be both posionous and edible, depending on dosage or certain parts of flowers can be poisonous and other parts are not.
The image classification model is most accurate when flower are close and central in the image.

Hyperparameters:

Batch Size:

32

Epochs:

10

Learning Rate:

1e-4

What type of neural network/model? What functions are being used?

Classification model!

104 Flowers: Garden of Eden Kaggle Dataset
104 Classes
192x192x3
- 104 Training Images
- 7382 Test Images
- 104 Validation Images
